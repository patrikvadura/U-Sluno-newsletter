What happened in U&Sluno in 2020?

Patricia Jakešová hosted New Retail Operations at the Retail Summit in Prague.

At the Prague office, we adopted a new four-legged colleague.

We had sewn 400 masks in company colors.

In emergency mode, we worked from home and it worked.

We launched a new website uslunorobotics.eu where we show what autonomous robots can help with.

In June, we broke last year's record and together we rode a bike, ran and walked 3 365 km.

At the All Staff Meeting, we took social distance seriously.

We recorded 36 Czech and Russian podcasts about the retail world.

Our robot Emil was placed in the TOP 3 as the Technical and Technological Innovation of the Year LOG-IN Award.

The employees supported the Bílá pastelka collection for the visually impaired people with 4 665 CZK.

U&SLUNO attended conferences in Ukraine.

And we participated in the Food Collection of 168 kg of durable foods and drugstores.

We have something to look forward next year. We will celebrate 30 years.
